### My take on it

I like it, one skein and it knits up decently quickly - even for a slower knitter like me. The only things you need to know are knit, purl, k2tog (knit 2 together), and working with either circulars (yay magic loop) and DPNs or just DPNs. The pattern calls for US 11 (8mm).

## Just Follow the Pattern

For the first time through, mistakes were made. The ribbing was offset at the bottom, and I think I missed a couple of the decreases. It's ok though, it was a test and for me so I don't mind the wonky look. Oh, and the picture shows it inside out:
