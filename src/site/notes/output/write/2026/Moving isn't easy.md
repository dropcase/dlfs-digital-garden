## It's Not Always Difficult, Either

Finding a place to live isn't easy, at least in the last several years. Found one last year, then in need of more space already so I had to find another spot.

The overall movement piece hits though. It's springtime and there's a lot of movement outside - trees and plants are back in action, more birds are back, and people seem to be out of hibernation too.

Me? I know I have a lot of lifting to do in the near future, and trying to make sure I'm ready for it by moving myself around.

Almost everything is about movement. Get out of bed, get to your desk, get in your car, all around moving either yourself or something else from one place to another. Even moving thoughts from your mind to paper or a digital format.

Not sure if I really have a point to this entry. It's more about noting what I've thought about for many years and noticing it in a more focused way at the moment.
